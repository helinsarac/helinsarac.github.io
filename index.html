About Me

Hello, I'm Helin, 24 years old and currently studying Digital Data Strategy at Hyper Island, a creative business school. I always had an ability to work with technology, starting pretty visual with photography I then appliyed for a more academic education at Stockholm's University, which I really enjoyed, but I found myself lacking a creative aspect to it. During an amazing time in Istanbul where I did an exchange semester I applyed for a program at Hyper Island, and looking back I don't think I could have made a better decision than this. When I'm not studying or working on projects I love to spend my time drinking tea, travelling, painting and watching anime. Summer of 2016 is the time for me to finish school and I'm looking for an experience of a lifetime.
